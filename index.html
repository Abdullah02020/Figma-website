Process

Initial Consultation

This step involves a discussion between the design team and the customer to determine the customer's needs, goals, and preferences.

Concept Development

The design team will then develop initial concepts for the presentation design based on the customer's input and branding guidelines.

Revisions

The customer will have the opportunity to review and provide feedback on the initial concepts, and the design team will make revisions as needed to ensure the final design meets the customer's expectations.

Final Delivery

Once the design is approved, the final presentation files will be delivered to the customer in the requested file formats (such as PowerPoint, Keynote, or Google Slides).

What services does your presentation design service offer? +

Our presentation design service offers custom design and professional templates for presentations in PowerPoint, Google Slides, and Keynote. We also provide revisions and support to ensure you get the best results for your presentations.

How much does your presentation design service cost? +

Our presentation design service has three different pricing tiers, starting at CHF 49/month. Our Basic tier includes 2 custom designed presentations per month, our Pro tier includes 4 custom designed presentations per month, and our Enterprise tier includes 8 custom designed presentations per month.

Our presentation design service can design a wide range of presentations, including pitch decks, marketing presentations, training presentations, and more. We can work with your content to create a custom design that aligns with your brand and meets your specific needs.

No, you do not need to have any design skills to use our presentation design service. Our team of professional designers will handle all the design work for you, ensuring you get high-quality results without any hassle.
